Bio

Oscar officially joined the club as a scholar in July 2019 from Norweigan side Vålerenga.

A Norweigan youth international, Oscar was an integral part of the U18’s winning back-to-back league titles in 2019/20 and 2020/21.

An incredibly technically gifted midfielder, capable of playing centrally as well as out-wide, Oscar was promoted to the EDS squad for 2021/22.

He ended the season as EDS Player of the Year, following 26 appearances in the successful Premier League 2 campaign.

He joined Pep Guardiola‘s squad for their pre-season tour in Asia where he showcased his ability and strong work ethic.
